Yumbolt

This marvelous plant is come from Humbolt californian mountains, it is the result of auto-polinize one elite clone that has won various cannabic awards.

It is a strong plant, bug resistant and mold too, although you mist prevent oidio.
It blooms in many branhces and long orange buds. Outdoor it finishes end october, int small pots it can be ready in october begining.

It flavour it is pure citric, from the first to the last hit has the same intense flavour. High production and heavy and hard buds.

Genetic = Californian

Indoor Flowering: 65 days 12/12.

Outdoor Flowering: First October weeks.

Indoor Production: 500-600 gr x m2.

Outdoor Production: up to 1 kg x plant ( in ground ) even more.

Smell and Flavour : It smells like sour tangerine, very strong. Tastes sour too, very deep taste from the first hit to the last. You will love it!

Efect: Cerebral, do not stone you direct to the sofa, great to b e creative and work in artistic way, very powerful.

Critical Express

We present you our first stabilized femenized Autoflowering strain, made in 4x4 Seeds, Kritikal Bilbo descendant but Autoflowering.
After crossing kritikal Bilbo with several atutomatic plants, (Syrup was one of them) a selection process and back-crossing during 7 generations we could give most of the fenotipical characteristics of the original non-auto plant, in this case the famous and awarded Kritikal Bilbo.

Ultra-fast blooming period thanks to the syrup ascendence, Critical Crack Auto is ready in just 2 months (55 days the fastest ones) if you grow it outdoor and small pots (max 7L), you lose some production. If you use huge pots outdoor(or in ground) or you grow it indoor with max light period you could harvest up to 120 gr.

Genetics: Kritical Bilbo x Syrup

Indoor Flowering: 60-65 days from germination.

Outdoor Flowering: in 7L pots: 60-75 days. In ground: 70-85 dias.

Indoor Production: 500-600 gr x m2

Outdoor Production: up to 120 gr x plant

Smell and Flavour : Sweet, like it parental Bilbo, but not so over-sweet. Tastes of pine and wood.

Efect: Relaxing effect, medium high power.

Tutti Frutti Ya

Our all star plant, this jewel of cannabis evolution is a "delicatessen". TuttiFrutti come from crossing several sweet and fruity plants, crossing and crossing the most vigouros and sweeter plants during 8 generations.

Outdoor it grows in 10 weeks, taking longer if you grow it in the ground. This marvelous plant, looks great because of its colors, when it starts to ripen her color go out and you can see pinks and purples tones and shades resulting in a pleasant visual effect.

Inoor it takes no longer than 10 weeks, it can produce up to 50 gr per plant if she has enough place and enough substrate, being more typical 30-35 gr per plant. You must have a good ventilation if you want to keep sane your buds, keeping mold away.

Genetics: Californian

Indoor Flowering: 65-70 days from germination.

Outdoor Flowering: in 7L pots: 65-70 days. In ground: 75-85 dias.

Indoor Production: 500-700 gr x m2

Outdoor Production: up to 200 gr x plant

Smell and Flavour : Sweet, sweet very sweet, almost sickeningly sweet. Smelling like candy and marshmallow and some of them smells exactly as water-melon and/or melon lollipop.

Efect: Relaxing effect and euphoric at the same time, makes you silly laugh, medium-high effect. Really good high to be creative.
